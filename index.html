JUSTIN TRUDEAU: THE 23RD PRIME MINISTER OF THE CANADA

BIOGRAPHY

Justin Trudeau born on December 25, 1971, is the 23rd Prime Minister of Canada and leader of the Liberal Party.Son of the former Prime Minister Pierre Trudeau, Justin entered politics after a career in education.Elected as Member of Parliament for Papineau in 2008, he became Liberal Party leader in 2013. Trudeau's 2015 election as prime Minister signaled a resurgenece of progressive politics in Canada. His tenure focuses on issue like climate change, gender equality, and reconcilation with Indigeous peoples. While praised for his charisma and inclusion leaderships, Trudeau has faced criticsm over ethics and environmental policy. As a prominent global figure, Trudeau continues o shape Canada's role on the whole stages.

What is Justin Trudeau's stance on immigration? Trudeau has generally been supportive of immigration and advocated for a welcoming approach.
